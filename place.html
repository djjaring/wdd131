Discover Manila

Manila is a vibrant coastal capital city known for its historic Intramuros, colorful jeepneys, delicious street food, and breathtaking sunsets over Manila Bay.

It blends Spanish colonial heritage, modern skyscrapers, and the warm hospitality of the Filipino people.

About Manila

Manila is the capital of the Philippines, located on the eastern shore of Manila Bay. It is part of the National Capital Region (NCR) and serves as the country's economic, political, and cultural hub.

Visitors can explore historic sites like Intramuros and Rizal Park, shop in massive malls, and enjoy local favorites like halo-halo and taho.
