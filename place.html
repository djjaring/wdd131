Discover Manila

Manila is a vibrant city known for its rich culture, historic Intramuros, colorful jeepneys, and breathtaking sunsets.

Experience a unique blend of Spanish colonial history, Filipino hospitality, and modern urban life.

About Manila

Manila is the capital of the Philippines, located along Manila Bay. It is a bustling center of culture, commerce, and history.

Popular attractions include Intramuros, Rizal Park, National Museum, Binondo, and the stunning Manila Baywalk.

Quick Facts
